Entrees

Penne alla Christi

Ingredients

  • 12 Chicken Tenderloins
  • 2 T olive oil
  • 2 T diced garlic (more if you like garlic)
  • 1/2 cup sliced sun-dried tomatoes in oil
  • 2 jars marinara sauce
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1/2 c grated parmesan or parmesan romano blend cheese
  • 1 bag baby spinach (washed)

Preparation

  1. Cook chicken tenderloins until just cooked
  2. Remove from pan and cut into bite sized pieces
  3. Put 2 T olive oil into 8 qt sauce pan
  4. Saute garlic and sundried tomatos
  5. Add 2 cups heavy cream and cook on medium until just begins to bubble
  6. Add 2 jars marinara sauce (I use Ragu or Bertolli)
  7. Cook over medium heat until reduced stirring every couple minutes
  8. Stir in 1/2 c grated parmesan cheese
  9. Stir in spinach until wilted
  10. Stir in cooked chicken
  11. Cover and cook on low until pasta is ready
  12. Cook 1 lb. Penne or your favorite pasta ( I use small bowtie pasta)
  13. Drain pasta

Serve sauce over a bowl full of pasta. Great with italian bread for dipping in sauce.
Serves 6

Sides

Scrumptious Cheddar and Bacon Twice Baked Potatoes

Ingredients

  • 4 large pototoes
  • 1 3oz. bag bacon bits
  • 1 8oz. bag sharp shredded cheese
  • butter

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Wash potatoes, dry, pierce on top with knife, wrap in paper towel
  3. Microwave 5 mins, turn over
  4. Microwave till done, they will be squishy (about 5-7 mins more depending on the size of the potato)
  5. Cut a slice of the skin off the top of each potato
  6. Hallow out potatoes placing potato pulp in large mixing bowl leaving the skins intact
  7. Place skins on baking sheet
  8. Mash the potatoes in the bowl with a fork
  9. Mix in 1/2 cup softened butter and 1 cup cheese and stir till mixed well
  10. Place potato mixture inside the skins
  11. Top each potato with remaining cheese and bacon bits
  12. Place in oven and bake for 10 mins

Birthday Brownie Cake

Ingredients

  • 2 boxes Ghirardelli Double Chocolate Brownies
  • eggs
  • canola oil
  • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips (divided)
  • 1 tub Pillsbury Creamy Supreme Chocolate Fudge Frosting
  • Pam baking spray with flour (the blue can)(not cooking spray)
  • 2 9x9 glass baking pans

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to temperature listed on box for glass pans
  2. Spray bottom only of pans with Pam baking spray
  3. Make brownies as directed on box with the addition of 1/2 choc. chips for each
  4. Cook according to package directions for the minimum time listed
  5. When brownies are cool at least 20 minutes - ice one pan of brownies with 1/2 of the icing
  6. Loosen brownies in other pan and remove from pan and place on top of iced brownies
  7. Ice brownie cake with the rest of the icing

Delicious served with chocolate or vanilla ice cream
